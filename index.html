Introduction

What is this?

If you're here, you probably love the game of scrabble or are looking to start exploring this weird and wonderful world. In either case, accumulating resources to study and practice is particularly difficult since all available resources are scattered around the internet. We aim to solve this problem by providing you a one-stop destination for all your scrabble needs. We hope that this tool helps you find all that you're looking for and strive ahead on your way to becoming a scrabble champion!
